ONE STOP CENTER

The One Stop Centre (OSC) Scheme provides a range of services including police facilitation, medical aid, psycho-social counseling, legal counseling and temporary shelter to women.

NATIONAL CRECHE SCHEME

The scheme focuses on children of 6 months to 6 years, of working women in rural and urban areas who are employed for a minimum period of 15 days in a month, or six months in a year.
